Avenis 125 Overview

Suzuki Avenis 125 is a scooter available at a starting price of Rs. 89,760 in India. It is available in 3 variants and 5 colours with top variant price starting from Rs. 91,223. The Suzuki Avenis 125 is powered by 124cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 8.58 bhp and a torque of 10 Nm. With front disc and rear drum brakes, Suzuki Avenis 125 comes up with combined braking system of both wheels. This Avenis 125 scooter weighs 106 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 5.2 liters..
